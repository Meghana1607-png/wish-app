Who Can Donate

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: Typically 18-65 years (confirm local guidelines).
  • Weight: Minimum weight requirement (e.g., 50 kg/110 lbs).
  • General Health: Must be in good health and free of major illnesses.

Common Misconceptions

  • "I can't donate if I have a tattoo." You usually can after a waiting period.
  • "Blood donation is unsafe." Explain that sterile equipment is always used.
